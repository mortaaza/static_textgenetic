Flagship product

Axion Fleet

AI-Powered Municipal Fleet Management

Axion Fleet gives public-sector fleet teams a single intelligent platform to manage every vehicle and asset — from acquisition to retirement — powered by predictive AI that cuts downtime and extends asset life.

  • Asset Lifecycle — track every asset from purchase to disposal with full cost-of-ownership visibility.
  • Work Orders — automated, prioritized maintenance work orders that keep crews focused and accountable.
  • Predictive Maintenance — AI that forecasts component failures before they cause costly breakdowns.
