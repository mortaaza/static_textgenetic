ISC — Testing Stream

TextGenetic AI has been selected for the Government of Canada's Innovative Solutions Canada (ISC) — Testing Stream, a national R&D program that procures and tests pre-commercial Canadian innovations in real-world operational environments.

The Testing Stream evaluates late-stage innovations (Technology Readiness Levels 7–9) through federal R&D contracts — providing rigorous, third-party validation that a solution is proven and ready for market, with a direct Pathway to Commercialization.

Healthcare · Brain Health & Neuroanalytics

AI Care Coordination & Neuroanalytics Platform

For a multi-site specialized neurodegenerative-care provider, TextGenetic AI designed a two-part AI platform spanning Parkinson's, Alzheimer's, and cognitive-decline care — uniting fragmented patient, program, and assessment data into one coordinated, privacy-conscious view, and laying the foundation for advanced brain-health analytics.

  • Care coordination layer — a central registry with automated reassessment tracking, program & milestone monitoring, and clear follow-up ownership across sites
  • Digital biomarker & neuroanalytics foundation — structured longitudinal capture of gait, balance, freezing-of-gait, cognition, and medication-response signals for clinical pattern mining and biomarker-candidate exploration
  • Clinician-supervised clinical intelligence — AI-assisted assessment and documentation support with human-in-the-loop review; never autonomous clinical decisions
  • Research-ready & responsible by design — de-identified, federated-learning-ready data foundations built with privacy, data governance, and clinician oversight at the core
